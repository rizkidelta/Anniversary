Our First Date

After you forgave me for being such a jerk, I finally agreed to take you to see the flowers at Gardens by the Bay. I remember you kept going on about how much you wanted to go when I was sending you home after that night out with your friends — you were tipsy and happy, and it was honestly kind of cute. Who would've thought that little moment would end up marking the start of our relationship, haha.

I guess your prayers in Korea really did work, because somehow they led us to where we are today. It's been a journey with ups and downs, but I'm grateful we ended up here, together.
